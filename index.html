About

Preferred usage

This app is Chrome-only. Connect your synth via usb-c to your computer. The app detects the midi. You can start playing.

Computer keyboard fallback

If you don't have a way to connect your synth to the computer via usb midi, you can play notes using your computer keys.

For example: C4 is the key "A", C♯4 is the key "W", D4 is the key "S" …and so on.

Chord completion hints

If you're missing notes of a chord, the app will show which notes to add to complete the full chord.

Bookmark chords you care about

You can bookmark any chord by clicking the star icon next to its name. This lets you easily find and focus on favorites.

Copy full chord info

Click on the Copy button to copy the entire card contents to your clipboard, including the chord name, tones, voice-leading, and scales.

"Where next" section

This section suggests musically logical next chords you might go to from the current one.

"Voice-leading" section

This shows how to move smoothly from the current chord to the suggested next chords. It breaks down which notes you keep and which you add.

"Scale context" section

This shows which scales fit the chord and lists the notes of those scales.

Have fun!
